COEP’s Satellite Initiative (Onboard Computer Subsystem)
COEP’s satellite initiative i.e. CSAT has successfully completed the launch and mission objectives of its first satellite SWAYAM and is currently working on the second mission COEPSAT2(CSAT-2). Follow the link for more information: www.coep.org.in/csat/

  • PID Algorithm Implementation
    • PID is an error-based correction algorithm, a PID control is implemented in our satellite to regulate the current supplied to a reaction wheel and hence the orientation of the satellite
  • Nested Vector Interrupt Controller
    • Configured its NVIC system to define different interrupts and write Interrupt Service Routines (ISRs) for them

ACADEMIC PROJECTS
